Summer Tours

Available from Big Sky and West Yellowstone


Our small group tours offer a private tour experience with a by the person rate. Our largest group size is 6 people and not the 10 to 35 passenger vehicles the other tour companies use.


By using small vehicles and SUV’s we are able to pull over for most wildlife sightings that interest us, travel Yellowstone’s smaller roads which are closed to the larger buses, and figure parking in most locations. We know the traffic patterns, and our guides have done an excellent job the past couple of years of keeping our guests out of the lengthy jams that occur daily.


We do cost more, but we will see more, and you will enjoy an experienced guide as we have very little turnover with our staff. We are not out to be the biggest, just the best.


What makes us unique from most of the other companies, is the fact we do not own and operate gas stations, hotels, fly shops, taxi companies or other businesses, we just offer Yellowstone Tours. Most of our guide staff has a lengthy tenure in Yellowstone, with most guiding in the park year round and for more than 10 years. Small groups, with the most experienced guides, we invite you to join us to see the difference.


Lower Loop Adventure

This classic adventure visits the Fountain Paint Pots, Old Faithful Geyser, the Old Faithful Inn, Yellowstone Lake, the Lake Hotel, Hayden Valley, the Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone, Upper Falls, Lower Falls, Crystal Falls and Gibbon Falls.

7 days a week from mid April through early November.

Upper Loop/ Wildlife Adventure

Yellowstone’s north side is often over-looked, and truly shows the diversity of Yellowstone’s terrain and wildlife. This trip visits Rustic Falls, Mammoth Hot Springs, Upper Terrace Drive (closed to large vehicles), Undine Falls, the Petrified Tree (closed to large vehicles), the Lamar Valley, Tower Falls and Dunraven Pass.

7 days a week from mid April through early November.

Central Yellowstone

The Artist’s Paint Pots, Norris Geyser Basin, Beryl Spring, the Virginia Cascades, the Grand Canyon of the Yellowstone (north Rim), Lower Falls, Grand View and Inspiration Points, the Washburn Springs Overlook, Dunraven Pass and the Chittenden Road (if open).This tour is a little more active with more trails to explore.

7 Days a week from Memorial Day Weekend through the end of September.

Private Day Tours

We allow up to 7 people per trip and up to 11 hours from Big Sky and 9 hours from West Yellowstone. When your guide picks you up, give him/her an idea of what you want to do and see and you and your guide will tailor your trip on the way into the park. Choose sightseeing, a day hike, wildlife viewing, geyser gazing (for the biggest shows) or swimming or a combination of the above, we want you to see Yellowstone the way you want to see it
