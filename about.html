About Us

As a non-corporate owned business, Yellowstone Tour Guides operates with a sole focus in Yellowstone Park Tours. We are proud to be one of the few companies left focusing on what we do best: Yellowstone Tours.

Our small group tour system offers the chance for individuals and couples to have a private tour experience with a by the person rate. Of course private tours are available as well for those who want to customize their adventure. If you think about it, would you rather tour with a larger group of 10 to 40 people, or enjoy a small group of no more than 6 people with us?

We also offer multi day private tour vacation packages, where we help you with every last detail of your adventure from lodging, tours, and alternate activities such as whitewater rafting, hiking, wildlife watching, ski lift rides, horseback riding and so much more. These packages really are the best way to get the most out of your Yellowstone vacation with the help of our local expert staff for planning.

Unlike many of the other backpacking companies, we offer local guides who only guide in Yellowstone.
