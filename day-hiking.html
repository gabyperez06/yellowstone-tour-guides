Day Hiking

Get out, Hike, and Explore!


Yellowstone’s day hike tours get you into the backcountry, where It is wild, beautiful and great way to beat the summertim crowds.


Our guides are trained in Yellowstone’s backcountry, and will offer you the use of our bear spray (ages 13 and over), and instruction. Every trail has great scenery and we will tailor hikes to the group’s interests and abilities.7 days a week, year ‘round (conditions permitting)


Yellowstone is home to several large predators and over 1200 miles of trails. Our guides will provide you with bear spray use, instruction and the experience required for a safe trip through the backcountry. We will travel up to 2 hours from Big Sky or West Yellowstone to one of Yellowstone’s 96 trailheads for a 4 to 8 mile hike in the backcountry.
